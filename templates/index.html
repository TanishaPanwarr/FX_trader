WITHDRAWAL PROCESS AND CHARGES...!!!

Clients are always encouraged by FX Oxford to withdraw their profits and investments whenever they require. They have the freedom to withdraw funds at any time they want. FX Oxford aims to process the withdrawals in a maximum of 4 hours and in most cases clients receive their funds earlier but never later than 6 hours, Client can apply their withdrawals by clicking up the withdrawal request by logging into their respective account. Since the AML policy is strictly followed by FX Oxford we never process withdrawals to 3rd party accounts. Funds can only be transferred to the account under clients account. KYC documents must be submitted by the client and approved by FX Oxford compliance team in order to process the withdrawals. Clients can also withdraw the funds to their Neteller or Skrill accounts. FX Oxford will never claim any charges when processing withdrawals, Banks may have conversion charges/other charges based on their policies.
